Welcome to the Credit Risk Study

Thank you for participating in this research experiment! You’re about to help us understand how people make decisions about credit risk—a key factor in financial lending.

What to Expect: You’ll review 20 customer profiles, each with details like credit usage, payment history, debt, and income. For each, you’ll make an initial decision (e.g., “low risk” or “high risk”) based on this data. Depending on your group, you might then see predictions or graphs to refine your choice.

Your Role: Imagine you’re a loan officer. Your decisions will simulate real-world judgments. There’s no right or wrong—just use your best judgment based on the information provided.

Why It Matters: Your input will help us explore how additional tools (like AI predictions or visuals) affect decision-making. This could improve how financial institutions assess risk.
